Privacy Policy for Golf Grind

Last updated: July 11, 2025

Overview

Golf Grind is a golf practice tracking app that helps you improve your game. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Golf Grind does not collect any personal information, account data, or identifiable information. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

Data Storage

All golf practice data, scores, and app settings are stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any data that could identify you

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. Your golf practice information remains private and stored only on your device.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches from our servers.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the Golf Grind app from your device. This will permanently remove all stored golf practice data and settings.

Children's Privacy

Golf Grind does not collect any information from users of any age. The app is safe for children to use as it operates entirely offline.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy here with an updated "Last updated" date.
